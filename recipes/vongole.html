VONGOLE

Spaghetti Vongole; an Italian pasta dish containing white wine, olive oil, chopped parsley, garlic, chilli and of course, clams.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 Cup of white wine
  • 4 Garlic cloves
  • Chopped bunch of parsley
  • Pinch of chilli
  • 500g of shelled Clams
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • Sprinkle of salt & pepper to taste

STEPS:

  1. Steam clams in water on stove top
  2. Prepare your ingredients
  3. Put your pasta on to boil
  4. Heat ingredients in large saucepan
  5. When clam shells have opened they are ready
  6. Add cooked pasta to saucepan with ingredients
  7. Stir in together
  8. Add clams to saucepan
  9. Serve
