SPOOKY HALLOWEEN MUFFIN

TRICK OR TREAT? A delicious burst in your mouth explosion of exciting flavours and intriguing textures

INGREDIENTS

  • Canned corn
  • Buttermilk
  • Essence of frog breath
  • Edible flower arrangements
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Flour
  • Arsenic
  • Spinach

STEPS

  1. Put metal can of corn in microwave on high
  2. Pour gallon of arsenic in pot and bring to boil
  3. Slowly stir in flour and bring to a roux
  4. Add granulated sugar, essense of frog breath and spinach
  5. Let contents cool
  6. Form into a dough
  7. Put spoonfuls of canned corn encased in the muffin dough
  8. Put into muffin tray and in oven for 5 hours
  9. Once done and cooled add buttermilk over the top for a saucy dressing
  10. Top with edible flowers
